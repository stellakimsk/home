General Information on Acrylic Paint

Introduction to Acrylics

Acrylic paints are water soluable, but once put onto canvas or clothing, can prove difficult to remove. The texture of acrylic paints can be easily modified using different amounts of water.You can also add, gloss, sand, salt, etc to experiment more with texture. There are different types of acrylic paints, usually being that the more expensive professional quality paints having more texture and pigment while cheaper casual paint has less. Most acrylic paints tend to resemble oil paints, with the difference that it dries faster and can not be blended as easily. Being non-toxic, acrylics are usually used in schools, but can be used professionally and for household use. Acrylic uses different techniques including different paint coats and blending styles.
