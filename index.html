Introduction to Paints


In this website, you can learn about 3 different types of paint: Oils, acrylics, and watercolours. These paint mediums are used all around the world for both artistic and industrial use. Personally, I love using all three of these paints for artistic purposes. For now, here are some trustworthy brands I personally love to shop from for paint!

  • Artist's Loft
  • M. Graham
  • Martha Stewart
